Main rules of this application

  1. The main point of this app is to allow users with common types of mood disorders such as major depression, dysthymia (dysthymic disorder), bipolar disorder, mo od disorder due to a general medical condition, and substance-induced mood disorder to track their mood
  2. This application is designed to keep track of your mood using both objective and subjective approach.
  3. Subjective approach refers to a ordinary daily rating of your mood. Unfortunately sometimes it's hard to conduct good personal judgement so a neural network is implemented to provide more objective way to rate your day based on your daily mood description
  4. Your daily rating is on a scale 1 to 10, where 10 is the best and 1 is the worst.
  5. To keep it simple and tidy just one rating and one message is allowed to submit a day
  6. You can submit your day description (message) and your (daily rating) simultaneously or one at a time
Neural network model based on LSTM technology takes care of your rating based on your daily mood description. Your message is evaluated either as negative (0) or positive (1).
